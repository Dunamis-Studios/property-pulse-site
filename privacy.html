Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dunamis Studios ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use Property Pulse ("the App").

1. What We Collect

We do not collect or store any of your data on any external server. Property Pulse runs entirely inside your HubSpot portal as a CRM extension and serverless function hosted by HubSpot. The App accesses the following data only in real time, only within your portal, and only for as long as a page is open:

  • CRM property definitions for the record being viewed
  • Property history entries for tracked properties on the record being viewed
  • Owner directory entries (to resolve owner IDs and the author name on manual property changes)
  • Custom object schemas (to discover which custom object types exist in your portal)

This data is read directly from HubSpot's APIs by a serverless function running inside your portal, rendered into the CRM card, and discarded as soon as the page is closed. Nothing is transmitted to, logged by, or persisted on any Dunamis Studios server.

2. Where Settings Are Stored

Admin-configured tracked properties and per-property display settings are stored inside your own HubSpot portal as a HubDB table named property_pulse_config_v2. Users' personal tracked property additions are stored in the same table, keyed by HubSpot user ID so each user only sees their own selections.

Because the configuration table lives entirely inside your portal, you own the data and can inspect or delete it at any time from the HubDB area of HubSpot.

3. Data Sharing

We do not collect your data, so there is nothing to sell, rent, or share with third parties. The App does not contain any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry.

4. Data Retention

There is no external retention policy because there is no external data. When you uninstall the App, you may optionally delete the property_pulse_config_v2 HubDB table from your own portal to remove the stored settings.

5. Security

All App code runs inside HubSpot's infrastructure. Property Pulse uses the static access token HubSpot provisions for the app -- it never stores or transmits HubSpot credentials to any external system. All API calls between the App and HubSpot happen inside HubSpot's network over HTTPS.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your HubSpot portal at any time by uninstalling it from your Connected Apps. Because no data is stored externally, uninstalling immediately ends all access.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised date.
