1. Use of the App

Property Pulse is a HubSpot CRM app card that surfaces the change history of configurable CRM properties on record pages, along with a settings page for admins to pick which properties to track. You may use the App solely for lawful purposes and in accordance with these Terms and HubSpot's own terms of service.

2. HubSpot Integration

The App runs as a HubSpot CRM extension inside your HubSpot portal. By installing the App, you authorize it to read and write CRM property values, read property metadata, record property history, owner directory entries, and HubDB tables in your portal as described in the permissions screen during installation. Write access is used only when a user explicitly edits a property value through the inline editing feature on the card. You may revoke access at any time by uninstalling the App from your HubSpot Connected Apps.

3. Data

The App does not store, sell, or share your CRM data on any external server. All property metadata and change history is read in real time from HubSpot and rendered directly in the card. Admin-configured settings are persisted inside your own portal's HubDB as a table called property_pulse_config_v2, not on any third-party infrastructure.
